How will this work? I'm just talking into a phone?
Recording yourself speaking triggers the same stress response — racing heart, filler words, self-consciousness. The anxiety is real even without an audience.
Most people never practise because every opportunity involves other people. This removes that barrier so you can actually start.
The programme starts with simple exercises — just you talking aloud, no audience. Each phase raises the difficulty gradually. By Phase 6 you're practising job interviews and handling tough questions under pressure. It builds up so you're ready when it counts.
