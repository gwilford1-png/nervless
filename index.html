Phase 1
Understanding the Fear
Easy
  • 🧠Understand why your brain treats speaking like a threat
  • 🔍Map your personal triggers, symptoms, and anxiety history
  • 🚪Learn why avoidance makes fear worse — and what to do instead
Phase 2
The Body First
Easy
  • 🫁Control your nervous system with breathing techniques
  • 💪Release physical tension before you speak
  • ⚡Reframe adrenaline as energy, not danger
Phase 3
Gradual Exposure
Medium
  • 🪜Build an anxiety ladder from low-stakes to high-stakes
  • 🎯Practice introductions, opinions, and spontaneous answers
  • 📈Experience what your voice sounds like when you're not afraid
Phase 4
Changing the Narrative
Medium
  • 💭Challenge the catastrophic thoughts that fuel anxiety
  • 🔬Reality-test your fears with actual evidence
  • 🤝Build a kinder, more accurate inner voice
Phase 5
Finding Your Voice
Medium
  • ✨Discover what makes your communication style uniquely powerful
  • 📖Craft stories that land — and practise telling them
  • 😄Learn to use humour and authenticity without forcing it
Phase 6
Raising the Stakes
Hard
  • 👔Present confidently to senior people and sceptical audiences
  • 🔥Handle hostile questions without losing composure
  • ⏱️Perform under timed pressure and surprise prompts
Phase 7
In-the-Moment Tools
Hard
  • 🧩Structure answers on the spot using the PREP framework
  • 🔄Recover when your mind goes blank mid-sentence
  • 🎙️Steady your voice and control pace under real pressure
Phase 8
Performing Under Pressure
Extra Hard
  • 🎤Deliver full presentations with vocal variety and presence
  • 👥Read the room and adapt in real time
  • 🏆Perform at your peak when the stakes are highest
Phase 9
Maintenance
Ongoing
  • 📊Measure how far you've come with an updated anxiety ladder
  • 🔁Weekly sessions to keep your skills sharp
  • 🌱Build the long-term habits that make progress stick
